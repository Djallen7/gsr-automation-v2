# June Monologues (Ep026–Ep030) — Pass 1: Isolated Cues + Teleprompter Reformat

Date: 2026-06-09. Read-only sources. Nothing written to Basecamp/RDC/ProPresenter.
This is **Pass 1 only** — David's own graphics cues isolated, and the read text
cleaned to teleprompter form in David's house style. The creative **Pass 2**
(filling slots from the Graphics Tracker history, B-roll loops, AI where motivated)
is held for the next step.

## House-style rules applied (derived from May Ep021–025 + Mar/Apr teleprompters)
- Title/headline line is dropped from the read.
- Every pasted reference (video/article title, URL, timecode, "Use picture", angle
  brackets) is pulled OUT of the read into the cue list.
- `(PAUSE)` on its own line marks **a video clip that plays with its own audio**
  (David stops talking). Stills, article screenshots, and silent "just for visual"
  B-roll get **no** pause — the graphic rides under his narration.
- Spoken lead-ins ("Watch this:", "Take a look:", "Here is a clip…") are kept.
- `Picture this:` is treated as a cue **only** when an explicit marker/link follows
  (per Daniel 2026-06-09). It does not, on its own, trigger a graphic.
- David's wording is preserved verbatim. Where past practice softened a stale date,
  legal name, or brand, it is **flagged**, not rewritten.
- Cue #1 is always the **Title Graphic**.

Link type by href (from the HTML anchors): `youtube.com` → Clip/B-roll;
`wikipedia …/media/File` → exact Picture; news/journal domain → Article Screenshot;
`.mp3` → audio pull.
