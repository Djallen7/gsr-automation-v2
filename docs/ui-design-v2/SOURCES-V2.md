# Sources To Look Into (v2)

**Date:** 2026-06-07
Curated from the research teams, weighted toward what you asked for: expert UI people who build elegant, data-dense, dark, dashboard/production interfaces. All URLs verified to resolve. **Top picks for a non-developer are starred.**

---

## Expert people and studios (elegant dense product UI)
- **(star) Rauno Freiberg** — Vercel design engineer; his Web Interface Guidelines is the best concise quality checklist you can paste into build instructions. https://interfaces.rauno.me/ and https://rauno.me/
- **(star) Emil Kowalski** — design engineer (Linear, ex-Vercel), author of Sonner and Vaul; the reference on tasteful, reduced-motion-safe animation. https://emilkowal.ski/ and https://animations.dev/
- **Paco Coursey** — Linear design engineer; built cmdk (the command palette you will use). https://paco.me/ and https://github.com/pacocoursey
- **(star) Adam Wathan + Steve Schoger** — Tailwind and Refactoring UI; the most beginner-friendly experts for directing AI. https://www.steveschoger.com/
- **Karri Saarinen + the Linear team** — the benchmark for dense-yet-calm UI and the violet-on-near-black aesthetic. https://linear.app/now
- **Vercel Design / Geist** — the canonical modern dev-tool look (restrained type, mono numerals, single accent). Use the official system, not the archived community geist-ui. https://vercel.com/geist/introduction

## Books, essays, talks (the canon)
- **(star) Refactoring UI** — Wathan and Schoger. Spacing, hierarchy, color, depth. Best first book. https://refactoringui.com/
- **(star) Information Dashboard Design** — Stephen Few. The foundational text on at-a-glance dashboards and the common mistakes. https://www.perceptualedge.com/library.php
- **Edward Tufte** — data-ink and small multiples; the basis for compact data display. https://www.edwardtufte.com/tufte/books_vdqi
- **Laws of UX** — Jon Yablonski. Hick's, Miller's, Fitts's as plain cards; directly useful for ADHD reduction. https://lawsofux.com/
- **Nielsen Norman Group** — evidence-based usability and progressive disclosure. https://www.nngroup.com/articles/

## Dark-UI and violet/black craft (read these for the brand)
- **Sentry, "Building Dark Mode"** — how to do a disciplined dark color-token system; the source of the "violet-tinted neutral ramp" idea. https://blog.sentry.io/building-dark-mode/
- **Twitch, "Beyond Purple"** — why they re-engineered their purple for accessibility; how to keep violet legible. https://blog.twitch.tv/en/2019/12/03/beyond-purple/
- **Stripe gradient technique** — how a violet gradient is a hero moment, not a UI surface. https://kevinhufnagl.com/how-to-stripe-website-gradient-effect/
- **Radix Colors** — accessible 12-step violet/iris/plum scales with guaranteed contrast and dark mode. https://www.radix-ui.com/colors
- **OKLCH color picker** — Tailwind v4 is OKLCH-native; keep tints perceptually even. https://oklch.com
- **Purple gender/premium theory** — why deep + saturated + rationed reads premium, while light lavender reads feminine. https://treefrogmarketing.com/color-psychology-purple/

## Production/pipeline tools worth studying (your domain)
- **Frame.io** — saved-filter "Collections": one asset table, many live views. https://frame.io/features/workflow-management
- **Yamdu** — episodic/series production; the episode-vs-batch toggle. https://yamdu.com/en/for-productions/tv-series/
- **Autodesk Flow Production Tracking (ShotGrid)** — burndown of assets against a deadline. https://www.autodesk.com/products/flow-production-tracking/overview
- **StudioBinder** — stripboard schedule + call sheet; status as a delivery-receipt chain. https://www.studiobinder.com/
- **Basecamp Hill Charts** — the best "stuck vs cruising" glance ever shipped; 5 dots on a hill. https://basecamp.com/shapeup/3.4-chapter-13
- **Linear Cycles** — the time-boxed cycle with a pace line and auto-rollover; your month is the cycle. https://linear.app/docs/cycle-graph

## Command-center and status-page calm (the "open it and know" feel)
- **Better Stack status-page examples** (GitHub, Heroku, DigitalOcean) — the single master-verdict line and re-rank-not-add pattern. https://betterstack.com/community/guides/incident-management/status-page-examples/
- **Calm Technology** — peripheral vs focal, ambient indicators over alerts. https://www.calmtech.com/
- **Grafana / Datadog / Sentry dark dashboards** — premium dark data density without gamer neon. https://grafana.com/

## Component ecosystems + galleries (your stack)
