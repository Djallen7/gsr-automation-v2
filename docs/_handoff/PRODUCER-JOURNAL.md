# PRODUCER-JOURNAL.md — Daniel's brain dumps, raw and dated

This is the raw intake for Daniel's daily producer brain dumps (his words: "ADHD rants
and rambling"). It exists so that NOTHING he says is ever lost, and so interpretation
is a separate, inspectable step instead of something that silently mangles his words.

**Rules (binding on every session that touches this file):**
1. **Append-only, dated, raw first.** Every dump lands verbatim (or as close as the
   medium allows), with date, time if known, and source (chat dictation, note, etc.).
   Never paraphrase a dump in place of recording it.
2. **Interpretation is a second step.** Each entry may carry an EXTRACTED block below
   it: candidate facts pulled from the entry, each tagged with one of three layers:
   ALWAYS (stable fact), USUALLY (habit/pattern), NOW (current state of a task). Every
   extracted item keeps Daniel's underlying words as its receipt.
3. **Contradictions are data, never errors.** When a new statement conflicts with an
   older extracted item, flag the pair as CONFLICT and keep both, dated. Recency wins
   for plain facts (a date moved). For routines and orderings, a conflict usually means
   "it depends on something" and stays open until the pattern shows itself or until it
   blocks real work, and only then does it become ONE question to Daniel. Never
   silently overwrite, never silently keep both unflagged.
4. **Echo receipt after every dump:** reply to Daniel with at most 5 lines: what was
   logged, what changed in the picture, what now conflicts. His one-line corrections
   get appended as their own entries.
5. **Never ask Daniel anything this journal already answers.**
6. Stable, repeatedly-confirmed items get PROMOTED to the month map / canon with a
   pointer back here. The journal itself is never canon.
